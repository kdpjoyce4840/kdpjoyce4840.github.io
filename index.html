Welcome! My name is Krystal Joyce, and I’m excited to share my professional journey with you. Here you’ll find my background, projects, and reflections as I continue developing skills in instructional design and technology.

Professional Background

I hold a BSE in Elementary Education and have 10 years of experience teaching across multiple grade levels. I later transitioned into nonprofit counseling, working with adolescents facing substance-use challenges and eventually serving as a supervisor. I currently work as a Level 1 Support Technician for SightView Software while pursuing my Master’s in Learning Technologies and Design at the University of Missouri. Outside of work, I am a proud mother of two children and enjoy blending family life with my professional journey.

Mental Health Interventions for Adolescents Raised by Grandparents

Course: ISLT 9450 · Research Methods

Date: April 2025

This project explores how being raised by grandparents due to parental incarceration or substance use impacts adolescents’ mental health. It applies an explanatory sequential mixed-methods design: quantitative SDQ scores followed by qualitative interviews to uncover lived experiences.

  • Identified emotional, behavioral, and academic challenges in grandfamilies
  • Explored systemic barriers: custody, finances, and access to care

Mental Health Interventions for Adolescents Raised by Grandparents

Course: Research Methods in Information Science and Learning Technologies (ISLT 9450)

Date: April 2025

This project explores how being raised by grandparents due to parental incarceration or substance use impacts adolescents’ mental health. It applies an explanatory sequential mixed-methods design, beginning with quantitative SDQ mental health scores followed by qualitative interviews to uncover lived experiences.

  • Identified emotional, behavioral, and academic challenges in grandparent-led households
  • Explored systemic barriers: custody, financial strain, access to care
  • Applied Phenomenology and Resilience Theory frameworks
  • Proposed school-based interventions, community resources, and structured therapies (e.g., ACT)

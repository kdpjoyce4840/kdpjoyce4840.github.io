Grandfamilies Study (Adolescents Raised by Grandparents)

A mixed-methods proposal focused on adolescents raised by grandparents due to parental incarceration and/or substance use. The project explores learner context, equity, and supports that improve outcomes for youth in custodial grandparent households.

  • Defines research questions and learner needs
  • Outlines qualitative + quantitative data collection
  • Maps ethical considerations and accessibility
  • Connects findings to instructional recommendations

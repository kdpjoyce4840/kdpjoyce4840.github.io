Grandfamilies Study (Adolescents Raised by Grandparents)

ISLT 9450 · Research Methods · April 2025

Mixed-methods proposal on adolescents raised by grandparents due to parental incarceration or substance use. Explanatory sequential design: quantitative SDQ → qualitative interviews to surface lived experience.

  • Identifies emotional, behavioral, and academic challenges in grandfamilies
  • Explores systemic barriers (custody, finances, access to care)
  • Uses Phenomenology & Resilience Theory frameworks
  • Proposes school-based interventions and community supports

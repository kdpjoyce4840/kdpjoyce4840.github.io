Grandfamilies Study (Adolescents Raised by Grandparents)

Course: ISLT 9450 · Research Methods   |   Date: April 2025

Mixed-methods proposal on adolescents raised by grandparents due to parental incarceration or substance use. The design uses an explanatory sequential approach: quantitative SDQ data followed by qualitative interviews to surface lived experience in more depth.

  • Identifies emotional, behavioral, and academic challenges in grandfamilies
  • Explores systemic barriers (custody, finances, access to care)
  • Uses Phenomenology & Resilience Theory frameworks
  • Proposes school-based interventions and community supports

L1 Ticket Handling Training Guide

A practical training guide for Level 1 Support ticket handling at SightView Software. It outlines a complete workflow from initial intake to resolution, emphasizing accurate documentation and clear communication with clients.

The guide walks new agents through verifying practice details and account status, gathering evidence, reproducing issues, and writing concise but thorough ticket summaries. It also covers escalation criteria, follow-up expectations, and consistent use of HubSpot fields to keep tickets traceable and transparent.

L1 SOP – Fixing MVE Texting Issue via LogMeIn

A standard operating procedure for resolving texting failures in My Vision Express (MVE) when the issue is workstation-specific. The SOP shows L1 agents how to connect via LogMeIn Rescue, transfer the repair file, and run it with administrative privileges on the correct machine.

It includes step-by-step instructions, file path details, and post-fix validation steps to confirm that texting is working again. The document also clarifies how to document the session (workstation, user, file details, and confirmation) and when to treat an issue as a broader outage that must be escalated to L2.

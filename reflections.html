What I’m Learning

This semester strengthened my focus on learner-centered design, accessibility, and actionable feedback loops. I’m integrating these practices into every project moving forward.

Top 3 Takeaways

  • Start with measurable outcomes and align all activities.
  • Design accessibly from the start—keyboard nav, alt text, color contrast.
  • Prototype early with real user feedback to de-risk design choices.

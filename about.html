Welcome! My name is Krystal Joyce, and I’m excited to share my professional journey with you. Here you’ll find my background, projects, and reflections as I continue developing skills in instructional design and technology.

Professional Background

I hold a BSE in Elementary Education and have over 10 years of experience teaching across multiple grade levels. I later transitioned into nonprofit counseling, working with adolescents facing substance-use challenges and eventually serving as a supervisor.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master’s in Learning Technologies and Design at the University of Missouri, where I’m building skills in instructional design, technology integration, and creating accessible learning experiences.

Reflections

My work is guided by a belief in the power of education and technology to create meaningful change. I strive to design learning experiences that are inclusive, engaging, and impactful for all learners.

About Me

Krystal Joyce • Learning Technologies & Design @ Mizzou • L1 Support Technician, SightView Software

Hi, I’m Krystal Joyce. I spent ten years teaching in public schools before moving into nonprofit counseling, where I supported adolescents and later managed a team. Now I work as a Level 1 Support Technician at SightView Software while earning my Master’s in Learning Technologies and Design at the University of Missouri. I love combining teaching and technology to create clear, helpful learning experiences. Outside of work, I enjoy gardening, creative projects, and time with my three kids.

Quick Facts

  • Current role: Level-1 Support Technician (My Vision Express)
  • Graduate program: M.S. Learning Technologies & Design (Mizzou)
  • Interests: instructional design, accessibility, digital media

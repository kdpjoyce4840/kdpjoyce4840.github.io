Professional Background

I earned my BSE in Elementary Education and spent over ten years teaching kindergarten, first grade, and sixth grade. During that time, I focused on creating supportive, student-centered learning environments.

After teaching, I transitioned into nonprofit counseling, working with adolescents facing substance-use challenges and complex family situations. I later moved into a supervisory role, coaching other counselors and supporting program growth.

Today, I work as an L1 Support Technician at SightView Software, where I help optometry practices use My Vision Express effectively. This role has strengthened my problem-solving, documentation, and ability to translate complex processes into clear, actionable steps.

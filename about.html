Professional Background

I hold a BSE in Elementary Education and have over 10 years of experience teaching across multiple grade levels, including kindergarten, first grade, and sixth grade. During this time I focused on building strong relationships with students and creating learning environments where each student felt seen and supported.

I later transitioned into nonprofit counseling, working with adolescents facing substance-use challenges and complex family situations. In this role I provided direct counseling support and eventually moved into a supervisory position, coaching other counselors and helping shape program practices.

Today, I work as an L1 Support Technician at SightView Software, supporting practices that use My Vision Express, an optometry EHR. This role has strengthened my technical problem solving, documentation, and ability to translate complex processes into clear, actionable steps for others.
